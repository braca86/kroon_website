Now it's time to connect everthing with nymea

What we did

In collaboration with guh team, we help conceptulize, design and think throught UX of their admin application, a web based app that enables guh customers to offer connected services to their end users. This application features very complex and rich functionality that allows for user tracking, setting up connectivity logic, services management as well as usability stats tracking.

Complex, yet intuitive UX to Connect everything
Weather you are garage door manufacutrer, produce audio equpment or you are in security bussiness, now you can offer IoT feature to your customers throught your deivces and services.
Usage examples of guh system
Dasboard to monitor devices, users and information
With customizable widgets one can set up a view to easly monitor all relevant information and have an easy access to more details view of each device, service or users as well as revenue and usage stats.

Plugins marketplace offer endless possibilities for connecting prodcuts and services
One can browse pottentially large number of plugins, that offer varius connected services. With the idea of open API, nymea is looking to enrisch peoples IoT experince by providing 3rd party plugins and in that manner provide the ability to connect alsmot anything.
